About The Greens Lounge

The Greens Lounge is a carefully curated space where comfort, sound, and service harmonize. The open layout allows guests to relax, connect, and enjoy each moment.

Calm and cosy during early hours, it evolves into a vibrant nightlife destination with expertly crafted cocktails, premium spirits, and live music performances.
